# 🚀 Nafs — Growth Strategy

Parent: [[Nafs]]

---

## Core Insight
The Muslim audience is cluster-based and trust-driven.
Creator outreach outperforms algorithmic channels.
One trusted Muslim creator recommending Nafs = thousands of installs.

---

## Phase 0 — Pre Launch
- Build audience before app is live
- ASO (App Store Optimization) — keywords, screenshots, description
- Social presence established
- Teaser content showing the UI and concept

---

## Phase 1 — Launch Channels
- Muslim micro-creators on Instagram + TikTok
- Reddit: r/islam, r/productivemuslim
- Telegram + WhatsApp Islamic groups
- ProductHunt launch
- Word of mouth in Muslim communities

---

## Phase 2 — Growth
- Ramadan campaign — biggest Muslim app moment of the year
- Long-tail SEO — "Islamic self improvement app", "muhasabah app"
- Referral mechanic via shareable weekly insight cards
- Mosque + Islamic school partnerships
- Meta ads targeting Muslim demographics

---

## Content Strategy
- Show the UI — calm, premium, different
- Show the Intelligence Engine output (anonymized)
- Speak to the pain: "tired of streak-based apps that make you feel guilty"
- Islamic creator partnerships — authentic, not paid-feeling

---

## Key Differentiator In Marketing
Position against habit tracker apps, not prayer apps.
Target: Muslims who already use productivity tools but want something
that understands their deen, not just their to-do list.

---

## Ramadan Strategy
- Ramadan is the single biggest acquisition moment
- Pre-Ramadan: build waitlist
- During Ramadan: push premium — highest motivation period
- Post-Ramadan: retention focus — "keep the momentum"
