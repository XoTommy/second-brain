# 💰 Nafs — Monetization

Parent: [[Nafs]]

---

## Tier Structure

### Free Tier
- Daily logging (salah, Quran, dhikr)
- Signal capture (mood, energy, discipline, distraction)
- Heatmap view
- Basic Reflections history
- Friday-aware UI
- Context-aware return messages

### Premium Subscription
- Intelligence Engine — weekly AI insight report
- Monthly pattern summaries
- PDF export of reflections
- Early access to new features
- Price: TBD (region-based pricing planned)

### One-Time Purchases
- Premium themes
- PDF export (if not bundled in subscription)

---

## Pricing Strategy
- Tier 1 (US/UK/EU): Full price
- Tier 2 (Middle East/SEA): Reduced
- Tier 3 (India/Pakistan/Africa): Accessible price point
- Early Supporter Lifetime Access: One-time timed offer at launch

---

## Monetization Ethics
- Free tier must be genuinely useful — not crippled
- Premium adds depth, not basics
- Never manipulative — no guilt-based upsells
- No ads — ever
- User trust is the most valuable asset

---

## Revenue Model
- Primary: subscription recurring revenue
- Secondary: one-time theme purchases
- Launch spike: lifetime access offer
- Long term: Ramadan campaign drives major installs + conversions
