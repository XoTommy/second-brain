# 🧠 Nafs — Intelligence Engine

Parent: [[Nafs]]

---

## What It Is
The hero premium feature of Nafs.
A weekly AI-powered insight report that reads the user's
worship logs, signals, mood, energy, discipline, distraction,
and consistency patterns — then delivers calm, private,
emotionally intelligent self-accountability insights.

---

## Why It's The Hero Feature
- Nothing like it exists in the Islamic app space
- Turns raw logging data into meaningful self-awareness
- Feels like a wise, private mirror — not a coach, not a judge
- Justifies the premium subscription on its own

---

## What It Reads
- Salah consistency (which prayers, which days)
- Quran sessions logged
- Dhikr logged
- Mood signal (1–5) patterns
- Energy signal (1–5) patterns
- Discipline/Intention signal (1–5) patterns
- Distraction signal (1–5) patterns
- Day of week patterns
- Missed days and comeback behavior

---

## Output Style
- Calm, not alarming
- Honest, not harsh
- Specific to the user's actual data
- Never generic advice
- Never preachy or lecturing
- Feels like insight, not judgment

---

## Example Insight Tone
"Your Fajr consistency was strongest mid-week.
Your energy tended to drop on days you logged higher distraction.
This week had more intentional days than the last."

---

## Privacy Rules
- All data stays on device
- AI insight generated locally or via secure API
- Never shared, never sold, never used for ads
- User must feel completely safe logging honestly

---

## Delivery
- Weekly report — available every Friday or Monday
- Premium only
- Notification to prompt user to read their report
- Monthly summary for long-term subscribers
