## Growth Strategy

### Primary Channel: Pinterest
- Pinterest is the perfect channel for wallpapers
- Pin every wallpaper individually with Islamic keywords
- Pins have extremely long shelf life — compound over months
- Each pin links to app store listing

### Secondary Channels
- Instagram — post wallpapers as content
- TikTok — "Islamic wallpaper pack" aesthetic videos
- Reddit: r/islam, r/MuslimLounge, r/androidwallpaper

### ASO (App Store Optimization)
- Keywords: Islamic wallpaper, Muslim wallpaper, Arabic wallpaper
- Screenshots show the aesthetic clearly
- Description emphasizes minimal + premium
