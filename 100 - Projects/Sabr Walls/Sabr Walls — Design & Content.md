# 🎨 Sabr Walls — Design & Content

Parent: [[Sabr Walls]]

---

## Aesthetic Direction
- Minimal — no decorative overload
- Editorial — feels curated, not mass produced
- Premium — dark backgrounds, gold typography, intentional spacing
- Inspired by high-end design, not generic Islamic app aesthetics

---

## Wallpaper Categories
- Arabic calligraphy (minimal, clean)
- Quranic ayat (exact text, beautiful typography)
- Nature + Islamic quote overlays
- Dark abstract + gold text
- Geometric Islamic patterns (minimal version)

---

## Content Rules
- Arabic text must be exact — verified sources only
- No unverified quotes attributed to Islam
- No kitsch or overly decorative styles
- Every wallpaper passes a "would I use this?" test

---

## App UI
- Clean browse/grid view
- Filter by category
- Preview full screen before download
- One tap download/set as wallpaper
- No login required for free tier

---

## Quality Bar
- Every wallpaper must feel like it belongs in a design portfolio
- Reject anything that looks AI-generated and generic
- Curated — 50 excellent wallpapers beats 500 mediocre ones
