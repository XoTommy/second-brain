# 💰 Sabr Walls — Income Potential

## Assumptions
- Premium subscription: $1.99/mo or $9.99/year
- Themed wallpaper packs: $0.99–$2.99 one-time
- App store cut: 15% (Small Business Program)
- Infrastructure: near zero — static image files
- Exchange rate: $1 = ₹84
- Indian income tax: effective 5% (low), 10% (mid), 20% (high)
- Conversion rate: ~3–5% of downloads convert to paid

## 📝 Notes
- Sabr Walls is the lowest effort highest passive potential project
- Infrastructure cost is near zero — images hosted cheaply
- Pinterest compounds forever — every pin is permanent traffic
- $1.99/mo is an impulse purchase — extremely low friction
- Ramadan pack, Eid pack, Hajj pack = seasonal revenue spikes every year
- 5,000 paid users = less than 0.05% of global Muslim smartphone users
- Build once, add wallpaper packs periodically, earn forever
- Lowest risk project in the entire portfolio
- Can run completely on autopilot after initial build
