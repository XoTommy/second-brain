## 🧭 What It Is
Phone connects to PC over WiFi via WebSocket.
Phone acts as a game controller with three preset layouts.
PC companion app runs in background, receives inputs, feeds into vJoy/ViGEm.
Games detect the phone as a real controller — not a workaround.

---

## Three Presets
1. Standard Xbox controller layout
2. Racing preset — Xbox layout + gyroscope tilt steering
3. Sim Rig preset — hero feature — touchable steering wheel + pressure pedals
