# 💰 Sim Racing Controller — Monetization

Parent: [[Sim Racing Controller]]

---

## Free Tier
- All three presets accessible
- 30 minutes per session limit
- Rewarded ad option: watch ad → get +30 minutes
- No layout customization
- No sensitivity customization

---

## Premium
- Subscription: $4.99/month or $29.99/year
- Unlimited session time
- Layout customization
- Sensitivity customization
- Early access to new presets

---

## Launch Offer
- Lifetime access: $49.99 one-time
- Available for 3 weeks at launch only
- Gone forever after launch window closes
- Creates urgency without being manipulative

---

## Monetization Logic
- Free tier is genuinely fun and usable
- 30 min limit hits naturally during a gaming session
- Rewarded ad is a fair exchange
- Premium removes all friction for serious users
- Lifetime offer rewards early adopters
