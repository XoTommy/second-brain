# 💰 Sim Racing Controller — Income Potential

## Assumptions
- Free tier: 30 min sessions, rewarded ads
- Premium: $4.99/mo or $29.99/year
- Launch lifetime: $49.99 (3 weeks only)
- App store cut: 15% (Small Business Program)
- Rewarded ad revenue: ~$0.02–$0.05 per ad view
- Infrastructure: minimal — WebSocket server only
- Exchange rate: $1 = ₹84
- Indian income tax: effective 5% (low), 20% (mid), 30% (high)

## 📝 Notes
- Sim racing is a passionate, spending community — premium conversion is high
- Product is self-demonstrating — one viral video can change everything
- $4.99/mo is extremely affordable for sim racers who spend $300+ on wheels
- Lifetime offer creates massive launch spike — use it wisely
- Rewarded ads are fair exchange — free users get value, you get revenue
- 1,000 paid users is realistic after one viral TikTok/Reels video
- Infrastructure cost is very low — Python WebSocket server is lightweight
- No force feedback in V1 — adding it in V2 justifies price increase
