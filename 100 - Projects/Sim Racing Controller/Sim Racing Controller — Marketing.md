# 📱 Sim Racing Controller — Marketing

Parent: [[Sim Racing Controller]]

---

## Core Insight
This product is self-demonstrating.
You do not need to explain it.
You just need to show someone playing Forza with their phone.
The reaction IS the marketing.

---

## Primary Channel: Short Form Video
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

---

## Content Strategy
- Screen record actual gameplay using the app
- Show the Sim Rig preset specifically — most visual, most impressive
- No voiceover needed — gameplay footage speaks for itself
- Caption: "I turned my phone into a sim racing wheel"
- Post consistently until one video pops

---

## Why This Works
- Sim racing community is large and passionate
- Mobile gaming community overlaps
- "Did you know you can do this?" content performs extremely well
- Product solves a real problem — not everyone has a $300 steering wheel

---

## Secondary Channels
- Reddit: r/simracing, r/ForzaHorizon, r/BeamNG, r/gaming
- YouTube tutorials: "how to use your phone as a racing wheel"
- Discord servers for sim racing games

---

## Launch Sequence
1. Build app to working state
2. Record clean gameplay videos
3. Post across TikTok + Reels + Shorts simultaneously
4. Ride organic traffic into launch week
5. Push lifetime offer during launch window
