# 💰 BuildCalc — Income Potential

## Assumptions
- Revenue: Google AdSense + Amazon affiliate + $0.99 PDF reports
- No app store cut (static website)
- Hosting: Netlify free tier — $0
- Exchange rate: $1 = ₹84
- Indian income tax: effective 5% (low), 10% (mid), 20% (high)
- AdSense RPM (revenue per 1000 visitors): $3–$8
  (construction niche is high CPC — US traffic especially valuable)
- Affiliate conversion rate: ~1–2% of visitors

## 📝 Notes
- BuildCalc is a purely passive income asset
- Every new calculator added = new SEO entry point = more traffic
- US traffic is most valuable for AdSense — construction niche CPC is high
- 10,000 visitors/month is very achievable with 10–15 calculators + Reddit posts
- Zero maintenance once built — true passive income
- PDF report via Stripe adds meaningful revenue with zero extra work
- Traffic compounds over time — plant calculators early, reap later
- Contractor lead gen (future) could 10x revenue at realistic traffic levels
