## Revenue Streams

### Google AdSense
- Display ads on calculator pages
- High CPC in construction/home improvement niche
- US traffic especially valuable

### Amazon Affiliate Links
- Link to relevant materials after calculation
- "You'll need X bags of concrete — buy here"
- Natural, non-pushy placement

### PDF Project Report
- $0.99 one-time via Stripe Checkout
- User gets a formatted PDF of their calculation
- Low friction, high conversion impulse buy

### Contractor Lead Gen (Future)
- Connect users with local contractors
- High value per lead in construction niche
