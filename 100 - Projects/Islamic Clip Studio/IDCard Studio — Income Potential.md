# 💰 IDCard Studio — Income Potential

## Important Note
IDCard Studio is a private internal tool for the family ID card 
printing business. It has no direct subscription revenue.
Revenue is generated by the family business charging schools 
per student for ID card printing.

## Assumptions
- Average students per school: 100–500
- ID card price charged to schools: ₹50–₹80 per card
- Printing + material cost: ₹15–₹25 per card
- No app store cut (private web app)
- No infrastructure cost (Firebase free tier initially)
- Indian income tax: effective 5–20%

---

## ❌ Worst Case — 5 schools, 2 batches/year

| Item | Amount (₹) |
|---|---|
| Cards printed (5 schools × 150 students × 2) | 1,500 cards |

## 📝 Notes
- Revenue depends entirely on how many schools the family business onboards
- IDCard Studio removes manual WhatsApp-based data collection
- Saves hours of admin work per batch — indirect value is huge
- Could potentially be turned into a SaaS product for other ID card
  printing businesses in future — massive untapped market in India
- No app store cuts since it is a private web app
- Firebase free tier handles early scale — infrastructure cost near zero
