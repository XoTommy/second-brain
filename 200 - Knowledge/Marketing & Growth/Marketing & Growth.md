# 📈 Marketing & Growth

---

## Core Insight
Distribution beats product every time.
A great product with no distribution dies.
A good product with great distribution wins.

---

## My Distribution Channels By Type

### Short Form Video (Sim Racing, True Form)
- TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- Best for self-demonstrating products
- One viral video can change everything
- Show don't tell — always

### Muslim Creator Outreach (Nafs, Islamic Clip Studio, Sabr Walls)
- Trust-based audience — creator recommendation = installs
- Muslim micro-creators outperform algorithmic channels
- One trusted creator > 10 paid ads
- Telegram + WhatsApp Islamic groups are underrated

### SEO (BuildCalc, Islamic Clip Studio)
- Long tail keywords, high intent
- Each page/calculator = passive traffic forever
- Compounds over time — plant trees early

### Pinterest (Sabr Walls)
- Perfect for wallpaper content
- Pins have extremely long shelf life
- Each pin = passive traffic for months/years

### Reddit (BuildCalc, Sim Racing, True Form)
- High intent, high trust community
- Give genuinely, link naturally
- r/DIY, r/simracing, r/islam, r/productivity

### App Store Optimization (All Apps)
- Title, subtitle, keywords, screenshots
- First impression before user even opens app
- Reviews compound over time

## Ramadan Strategy (Islamic Products)
- Single biggest acquisition moment of the year
- Pre-Ramadan: build waitlist + buzz
- During Ramadan: push hard — motivation is peak
- Post-Ramadan: retention focus

---

## Universal Growth Rules
- Build in public where appropriate
- Collect emails from day one
- Launch on ProductHunt for initial spike
- Talk to users — even 5 users teaching you is gold
