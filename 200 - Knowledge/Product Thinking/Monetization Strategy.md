# 💰 Monetization Strategy

---

## Revenue Model Hierarchy (Best to Worst)

1. Subscription — recurring, predictable, compounds
2. One-time premium unlock — low friction impulse
3. Affiliate/ads — passive but lower ceiling
4. One-time app purchase — good for simple tools

---

## Pricing Philosophy
- Free tier: genuinely useful, builds trust
- Premium: adds real depth, not just unlocks basics
- Regional pricing: capture global markets others ignore
- Launch lifetime offer: reward early adopters, create urgency

---

## Regional Pricing Tiers
- Tier 1 (US/UK/EU/Australia): Full price
- Tier 2 (Middle East/SEA/Turkey): 50% of Tier 1
- Tier 3 (India/Pakistan/Africa/Bangladesh): ~25% of Tier 1

## Launch Lifetime Access Strategy
- Offer once at launch — timed window (2–3 weeks)
- Never available again after window closes
- Creates genuine urgency without manipulation
- Rewards people who believed early

---

## Muslim Market Monetization Notes
- Muslim users will pay for products built specifically for them
- Trust is the currency — earn it, then monetize
- Ramadan is the single biggest revenue moment
- Avoid anything that feels exploitative of deen
