## Key Islamic Concepts For Products

### Muhasabah
Self-accountability before Allah holds you accountable.
The practice of reflecting on your deeds, intentions, states.
Core concept behind Nafs.
Not productivity. Not habit tracking. Spiritual self-awareness.

### Istiqamah
Steadfastness and consistency on the straight path.
Relevant to Nafs — consistency without guilt or pressure.

### Niyyah
Intention. Every action begins with intention.
Relevant to Nafs signal — "discipline" renders as "Intention."

### Tawbah
Repentance. Returning to Allah after falling short.
Relevant to Nafs missed-day recovery — no guilt, just return.
